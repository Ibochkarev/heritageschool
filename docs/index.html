HOW TO ENTER HERITAGE SCHOOL

Heritage School offers a supportive international environment and world-class education and care for children from 2 years old in Moscow.

We offer children more opportunities for their future. Thus we provide British (Cambridge) Curriculum by international teachers and Russian Curriculum.

Heritage School graduates get two diplomas — Cambridge and Russian — so they can any university in the world.
